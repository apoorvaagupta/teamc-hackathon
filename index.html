About Us

TEAMC-18

The International Conference, Towards Extensible and Adaptable Methods in Computing – TEAMC 2018, will be held during 26-28 March, 2018 under the aegis of Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology (NSIT), New Delhi. This conference will provide an excellent opportunity to researchers, practitioners and students to share ideas, discuss best practices and collaborate together to solve problems in convergent domains of computing that strive towards extensibility and adaptability.

TEAMC 2018 includes Agile Software Development, Data Management, Web Intelligence, Computing in Education and Machine Learning as its tracks.

Our Objective

The main objective of this hackathon is to sensitize students and professionals about existing problems of the under privileged and lesser developed sections of our society and providing a platform whereby they can proactively develop and showcase technological solutions for these challenges.
The Hackathon will be held in phases after completion of which worthy teams will get a chance to present their experience , product and report in the esteemed TEAMC conference

The Phases

Hackeam is a unique hackathon that proceeds through phases and leads to an international conference. As teams of multi-disciplinary talents go through these phases, they will experience the joys of visits to various places, interacting, reporting, designing, coding and paper writing. All teams wishing to participate in the hackathon must register themselves by the last date. Teams must strictly adhere to the rules as set by the organisers.

The competition culminates in 3 phases



PHASE 1

In this phase, the registered teams need to visit a Place of Interest (POI) which may be a rural or semi-urban area for the Rural Trek, a healthcare center for the Health Trek , a School or institution for Education Trek. Participants will document their visit using text, interviews, and photographs. The POI to be explored may or may not be one of the pre-designated areas listed by the organizers or chosen by the participants themselves.

PHASE 3

Teams will get an excellent opportunity to showcase their work in the prestigious International Conference TEAMC 2018 during March 26-28, 2018 (www.teamc18.com) at NSIT, in front of an international audience of researchers, industry leaders and government officials. Efforts will be made to carry forward potentially promising submissions so that they can be fully developed into working prototypes and products with the assistance of incubation centers.

Teams need to complete Part B their paper by adding experimental results and conclusion and submit the final paper comprising both Part A and Part B, by 10 Feb, 2018. The total length of the paper should not exceed 14 pages. At least one member of each team will have to register for TEAMC 2018 if they wish to present their paper.

The paper submissions will be reviewed and selected papers will be invited to present their papers. They will be published in an indexed Journal with ISBN. The best paper will be given the Best Paper Award in Hackathon category.
